273 - 232 BCE - Asoka’s reign brings Buddhism.

AFRICA

Ghana Empire
830 CE - START: Ancient Ghana was "officially" created

850 CE - Beginning of Gold and Salt Trade

1235 CE - END: Ancient Ghana becomes allies of the Mali Empire

Mali Empire
1230 CE - START: The empire was founded

1324 - 1325 CE - Mansa Musa goes on the Hajj - Brings Islam to Africa

1330 CE - Timbuktu becomes a trading center

1460’s CE - END: Spain took slaves and weakened Mali defenses

Songhai Empire
early 800 CE - Songhai have settled at Gao

1464 CE - START: Sonni Ali absorbs what is left of Mali into the Songhai Empire.

1468 CE - Takes over Timbuktu

1591 CE - END: The death of the Emperor Askia Daoud, a civil war of succession weakened the Empire.


CHINA

Han Dynasty
206 BCE - START: Liu Bang overthrows the Qin dynasty and starts the Han dynasty.

138 BCE - The Han dynasty begins to open the Silk Road

108 CE - The invention of Paper

220 CE - END: the last emperor was forced to abdicate



Tang Dynasty
618 CE - START: Li Yuan establishes the Tang dynasty

850 CE - Gunpowder invented

868 CE - World’s first printed book

907 CE - END: Zhu Wen ends the Tang dynasty.



Song Dynasty
960 CE - START: Emperor Taizu founds the Song dynasty.

1024 CE - Paper Money is introduced to the world.

1100 CE - Rebellions broke out throughout China because of the corrupted leaders.

1279 CE - END: Attacks from the Mongols, The Song dynasty ends.


JAPANESE FEUDALISM

Japanese Feudalism
1185 CE - START: End of the Heian Period

1274 CE - The Mongols first attempt to invade Japan.

1600 CE - END: Beginning of the Edo Period with Tokugawa Shogun.


ISLAM

Islamis Empire
630 CE - START: Muhammad returns to Mecca which becomes the center of the Islamic world.

1025 CE - The Canon of Medicine is published during the Islamic Golden Age.

1204 CE - The final decline during the Byzantine–Ottoman Wars.

1517 CE - END: The Caliphate in Egypt is overthrown by the Ottomans.


Mongol Empire

The Mongol Empire
1206 CE - START: Temujin assumes the title of Genghis Khan.

1221 CE - The Pax Mongolica begins

1227 CE - Genghis Khan dies.

1368 CE - END: The ming Dynasty reclaims China and the Mongol Empire officially ends.

Medieval Europe

1347 CE - The Black Death Starts in Europe.

1500 CE - END: The Medieval Period is usually thought to end when the Renaissance begins.
